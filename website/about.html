Latest Release

OpenWare 2.0 has been released with gameplay foundation upgrades, material improvements, optional Lua scripting, and background audio support.

Mission

OpenWare exists to provide a clean and understandable 3D engine baseline. It is designed for developers who want direct control over rendering, gameplay flow, and subsystem growth without fighting a huge framework.

What It Already Has

  • OpenGL renderer and scene draw pass
  • Rigidbody physics with collisions
  • Resource loading for OBJ/PPM
  • Debug and settings overlays

Expansion Direction

  • Animation system
  • Audio pipeline
  • Scripting and tooling
  • ECS architecture and networking
